Heterotic string compactifications

Here we list the tables with the maximally enhanced gauge groups realized in the

components of the moduli space of heterotic strings with 16 supercharges.

6 space-time dimensions:

  • Heterotic string on T4 (Narain component).
  • 6d Z2-triple (CHL string).
  • 6d Z3-triple.
  • 6d Z4-triple.
  • 6d Z5-triple.
  • 6d Z6-triple.

We only show the groups with maximal rank.

BCFG algebras always have short roots of length 2.

ADE simple algebras have roots of length 2 except when we write a superscript indicating half of the length.
