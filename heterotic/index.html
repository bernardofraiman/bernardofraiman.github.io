Heterotic string compactifications

Here we list the tables with the maximally enhanced gauge groups realized in the

components of the moduli space of heterotic strings with 16 supercharges.

9 space-time dimensions:

  • Heterotic string on S1 (Narain component).
  • 9d CHL string.
  • 9d (D16).
  • 9d (D16)Z2.

8 space-time dimensions:

  • Heterotic string on T2 (Narain component).
  • 8d Z2-triple (CHL string).
  • 8d (2D8).
  • 8d (2D8)Z2.

7 space-time dimensions:

  • Heterotic string on T3 (Narain component).
  • 7d Z2-triple (CHL string).
  • 7d Z3-triple.
  • 7d Z4-triple.
  • 7d Z5-triple.
  • 7d Z6-triple.
  • 7d (4D4)Z2.
  • 7d (4D4)Z2^2.
  • 7d (3E6).
  • 7d (3E6)Z3.
  • 7d (D4+2E7).
  • 7d (D4+2E7)Z2.
  • 7d (D4+E6+E8).

6 space-time dimensions:

  • Heterotic string on T4 (Narain component).
  • 6d Z2-triple (CHL string).
  • 6d Z3-triple.
  • 6d Z4-triple.
  • 6d Z5-triple.
  • 6d Z6-triple.
  • 6d (12A1)Z2^2.
  • 6d (14A1)Z2^2.
  • 6d (14A1)Z2^3.
  • 6d (15A1)Z2^3.
  • 6d (15A1)Z2^4.
  • 6d (16A1')Z2^4.
  • 6d (16A1)Z2^4.
  • 6d (16A1)Z2^5.
  • 6d (17A1)Z2^5.
  • 6d (18A1')Z2^6.
  • 6d (18A1)Z2^6.
  • 6d (19A1)Z2^7.
  • 6d (8A2)Z3^2.
  • 6d (9A2)Z3^2.
  • 6d (9A2)Z3^3.
  • 6d (4A1+4A3)Z2Z4.
  • 6d (6A1+4A3)Z2Z4.
  • 6d (6A1+4A3)Z2^2Z4.
  • 6d (6A3)Z4^2.
  • 6d (5A1+4A2+A5)Z6.
  • 6d (3A1+3A5)Z6.
  • 6d (3A1+3A5)Z2Z6.
  • 6d (3A6)Z7.
  • 6d (A1+A3+2A7)Z8.

We only show the groups with maximal rank.

Simple algebras always have short roots of length 2 except when we write a superscript indicating half of their length.
